Sentimentr

A sentiment analysis tool to see bias in media titles for 2020 presidential candidates

Find Out More

Sentimentr is a project by Nicholas Broad done in collaboration with SharpestMinds under the guidance of Tomas Babej. The goal of the project is to see which news agencies have a bias toward certain candidates, and if trends in popularity of candidates can be seen in title sentiments.

"(Valence Aware Dictionary and sEntiment Reasoner) - a lexicon and rule-based sentiment analysis tool that is specifically attuned to sentiments expressed in social media, and works well on texts from other domains."

(Long Short-Term Memory) - a type of artificial recurrent neural network that has feedback connections to allow for understanding of long sequences of words.

"(Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) - a model designed to pre-train on both left and right context in all layers and then be fine-tuned with just one additional output layer to create state-of-the-art models."
