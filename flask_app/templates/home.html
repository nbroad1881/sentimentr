Sentimentr

A sentiment analysis tool to see bias in media titles for 2020 presidential candidates

Find Out More

Sentimentr is a project by Nicholas Broad done in collaboration with SharpestMinds under the guidance of Tomas Babej. The goal of the project is to see which news news sources have a bias toward certain candidates, and if trends in popularity of candidates can be seen in title sentiments.
